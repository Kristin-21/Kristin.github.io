Speaker

Meet our speakers, who shared valuable knowledge and made our industry visits truly memorable. Their insights and experiences enriched our journey, making it both educational and enjoyable.

Speakers:
Arnel G. Gonzales

Enlightened us about the important role of Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical, and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA) in monitoring our weather, which helps us prepare for natural disasters and other events. His explanation deepened our understanding of how PAGASA's forecasts impact our daily lives, ensuring we are informed and ready to respond to emergencies effectively.

Dave Del Rosario

Mr. Dave Del Rosario enlightened us about how some parts of the plane work, enhancing our understanding of its mechanics and making our experience even more enjoyable.

Engr. Jerome D. Saddi

Engr. Jerome Saddi enlightened us about the role of their management systems, radar, and installed Closed-Circuit Television (CCTV) for surveillance and security purposes. His insights provided a deeper understanding of the logistics involved, ensuring efficient and secure operations.

Karla Legaspi

Ms. Karla Legaspi greeted us with a warm smile that made us feel right at home. She enlightened us about the importance of embracing challenges, emphasizing that by loving the problems we face, we can approach finding solutions with greater ease and positivity.

Adrian Francisco A. Salda

Mr. Adrian Salda enlightened us about how trains operate, explaining everything from their intricate systems to the materials used, such as the massive wheels. His insights gave us a deeper appreciation for the engineering behind trains. Moreover, he graciously allowed us to experience riding the train firsthand, which was both educational and exciting.

Rose Anne Caraan and King Jeather Cruz

Mr. King Jeather Cruz and Ms. Rose Anne Caragan enlightened us about the importance of innovation, encouraging us to explore new ideas and try out their products. Their explanations about how their products work amazed us, showcasing how these innovations can significantly simplify our lives, making tasks easier, more convenient, and saving us valuable time.

Brian Tanseng

Mr. Brian Tengthe Co-founder himself, enlightened us about his company, which we found to be the most relevant to our computer science course among all the companies we visited. His insights were particularly valuable, providing us with a deep connection to our field of study and offering meaningful insights that were highly beneficial.

Gretchel Listana

Ms. Gretchel Listana passionately explained that Hytec specializes in providing comprehensive power solutions and services. She emphasized their expertise in power generation, distribution, and management, highlighting their wide range of products such as generators and power systems.
