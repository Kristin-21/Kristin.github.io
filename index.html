BSCS Educational Tour

Join us on a Industry Visit by scrolling through your devices to explore the places and industries we have visited. Discover vibrant cultures, and gain insights into various fields we have encountered with just a swipe!

Day 1

Visit PAGASA Weather Station, First Aviation Academy, and Vessel Traffic Management System.

Here during our industry visit to PAGASA (the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration), we learned how they predict upcoming storms and other weather issues. They explained that typhoon names follow a cycle and are not reused. They also showed us the different tools they use to monitor rainfall and other weather conditions.

During our visit to FIRST AVIATION ACADEMY, we observed their meticulous approach to aircraft maintenance and discovered their training program's strong emphasis on leveraging technology. A standout feature was their state-of-the-art flight simulator, which allows students to safely practice flying. This tour underscored the academy's dedication to integrating technology into aviation education, ensuring both safety and effective learning outcomes.

Here we were able to see how to navigate the ship management system, including the radar, installed cameras, and other equipment, as explained by the speaker. While he didn't teach us how to navigate, he emphasized its importance in identifying illegal activities. We were also tasked with going upstairs to see the signal tower in person and to observe other cargo ships sailing in the bay.

Day 2

Visit QBO Innovation Hub and Packetworx Inc - IoT Technology Hub.

Here I have come to realized that we shouldn't stress over upcoming problems. Instead, we should embrace them. When we accept our problems, finding solutions becomes much easier. By using our skills and getting involved in our work, we're not just helping ourselves—we're also helping our community tackle different challenges.

Here, I was truly amazed at how people here come up with practical solutions to problems. They have a talent for making their projects both minimalistic and innovative, which is incredibly helpful to clients. Their approach really stands out and makes a significant impact.

Day 3

Visit Light Rail Transit Authority- Line 2 Depot.

Here we had the opportunity to ride the train with our professors. It was my first time on a train, and I was amazed at how smoothly it ran compared to buses, which tend to jolt over bumps. I really enjoyed the experience and found that I like trains a lot.

Day 4

Visit 98Labs, Inc. and Hytec Power Inc.

Here at first, I did not realize that Sir Brian was the co-founder of 98Labs until he started speaking about the company. Hearing about his life, especially how he overcame challenges during his studies and learned programming languages despite limited resources, was truly inspiring. It's amazing to see how successful he has become.

Here, we got to see robots in action and experience how they work. There were students doing on-the-job training, making their robots move and function. They even let us play with the robots using remote controls. I was also amazed at how they ensure students use advanced technology in their education.
